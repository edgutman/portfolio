Lyft

2023 • Tap to Add Credit Card (Patent Pending)

I tried Lyft's credit card scanner and it messed up the number so I had to type it in myself which is really annoying.

Adding a credit card during Lyft signup while trying to schedule a ride is a high friction event, so we leveraged NFC technology to allow riders to tap smart cards on their phone to automatically fill credit card fields.

Rider maintained preference for manual entry, but the tap to add option won out over the photo capture option by almost 2 to 1.

Affirm

2021 • Recommended Help Channels

I'm so frustrated trying to find answers to my question on the website, so I just Googled your customer support number.

Customers miss opportunities for easy self service within the Affirm Help Center, so we introduced a contact flow that suggests channels for the best outcomes based on the customer's query.

After launch there was a 31% increase in Affirm's CSAT score and a 32% decrease in support contacts.
